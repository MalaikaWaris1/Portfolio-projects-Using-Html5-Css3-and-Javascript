BBQ Jackfruit Sandwiches

  • Young Green Jackfruit: Found in Asian markets or the Asian section of many grocery stores, ensure it's in water, not syrup or brine.
  • BBQ Seasoning: A mix of brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and chili powder.
  • BBQ Sauce: Ensure it's vegan-friendly.
Menu Items:
  • BBQ Jackfruit Sandwich: Smoky, hearty, tender jackfruit in a whole grain vegan bun, topped with:
  • Avocado Slaw: A refreshing mix of avocado, maple syrup, lemon or lime juice, salt, and pepper, with shredded cabbage and carrots.
  • Roasted Salted Cashews: Adding crunch and flavor.
